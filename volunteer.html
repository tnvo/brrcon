Note that volunteering is not just a free ticket to the conference. You will be expected to provide services for BrrCON staff, attendees, and speakers. You may have to work long hours during the conference dates.

In exchange you will get access to the carnage of BrrCON like no others.You will get the satisfaction of giving back to the community and being a part of the machine that is BrrCON. This is not for the faint of heart. Please fill out the below form in its entirety to be considered


Volunteer Sign Up

Agreement to Terms of Volunteering:

By submitting the form below, I acknowledge that if I am selected to volunteer, I understand that I:
1. Will be expected to be onsite and available during assigned hours.
2. Will be expected to perform assigned task to the best of my ability.
3. May miss significant portions of presentations depending on volunteer assignment.
4. Am expected to be courteous and helpful to attendees, sponsors, and speakers.
5. Will be responsible for my own expenses (including travel).
6. Will be given a BrrCON staff badge.
7. Will have access to any amenities offered to BrrCON staff.
8. Am volunteering and will receive no compensation.
9. Am subject to dismissal at anytime due to poor performance and any such dismissal will result in revocation of my BrrCON badge.
10. May be assigned different assignments throughout the conference as need dictates.

By submitting, you agree to acknowledge that you have read the above and understand and agree to the terms as detailed in the Agreement to Terms of Volunteering.
